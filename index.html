About Ayna Clinic

Founded on the principles of medical excellence and aesthetic precision, Ayna Clinic is led by Dr. Bibisha Baaniya (MBBS, MD - BPKIHS), a board-certified specialist in Skin, Venereal, and Aesthetic diseases (NMC No. 17402).

Located in the heart of Biratnagar, our clinic provides comprehensive diagnostic and therapeutic solutions for all skin and hair concerns. We combine state-of-the-art laser technology with personalized treatment plans to deliver safe, effective, and world-class results for every patient.
